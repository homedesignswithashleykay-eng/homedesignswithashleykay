Before you book

Why do I need to fill the questionnaire first? ▼
Ashley reviews your answers, photos, and style preferences before the call so every minute is focused on your actual space — not on gathering basic info. It makes the call dramatically more useful.
Which packages include a call? ▼
The Revamp ($349–$499) and The Home Edit ($799–$1,299) both include a 30-minute design call with Ashley. The Refresh works entirely through the questionnaire — no call needed.
What happens on the call? ▼
Ashley walks you through her initial thoughts on your space, asks follow-up questions about your questionnaire answers, and aligns on the direction before she starts designing. You can also ask anything you want.
How do I pay? ▼
Ashley sends an invoice via Stripe or PayPal after the call. Full payment is due before the project begins. For The Home Edit, a 50% deposit is accepted with the balance due on delivery.
What if I booked The Refresh but want to upgrade? ▼
Easy — just email Ashley at homedesignswithashleykay@gmail.com and she will upgrade your package. The difference in price is all you pay.

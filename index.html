Before you book

Do I need to prepare anything? ▼
Nope — just show up. Ashley will ask about your space, style, and what's not working. Photos or inspiration images are welcome but not required.
What happens after the call? ▼
Ashley sends a package confirmation, invoice via Stripe or PayPal, and your client questionnaire. Once payment is received your project goes into the queue.
Can I skip the call and just book? ▼
Absolutely. Fill the questionnaire, submit photos, and Ashley gets started. Simple.
How do I pay? ▼
Ashley invoices via Stripe or PayPal. Full payment due before the project begins. For The Home Edit, a 50% deposit is accepted.
What if I'm not happy with my design? ▼
Every package includes revision rounds. Extra rounds can be added for $50 each.
I'm a renter — can Ashley still design my space? ▼
Yes, and this is a specialty. Ashley will design entirely around renter-friendly solutions — peel-and-stick, removable, deposit-safe.
